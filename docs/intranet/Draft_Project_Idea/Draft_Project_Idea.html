Project Idea - The AI School

An education platform for individual learning with an AI tutor

  • The platform analyses your preferences and suggests subjects that you may be interested in
  • Using our custom AI, students get learning program that go at their own pace
  • 3rd party education providers (uni, tafe, private college, music schools) can load their material into our servers for delivery to the customer
  • The AI is the most patient teacher ever!
  • The AI takes the learning outside. Field trips to collect samples for subjects like botany, biology. The AI knows where samples are likely to be found.

  • How can we better incorporate location awareness?
  • Deploy on Android and iOS. Anything else?
